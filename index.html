About Us

We are a grassroots political group based in Portland, Oregon. Our mission is to empower communities through direct action, mutual aid, and civic engagement. Join us in building a better tomorrow from the ground up.

50501 Portland specifically aims to unify local residents, organizers, and working-class people to confront systemic injustice, climate inaction, housing inequality, and state repression. We operate independently but in alignment with the national 50501 movement, emphasizing decentralized decision-making, transparency, and solidarity.

About the 50501 Movement

The 50501 Movement is a nationwide grassroots initiative that began in early 2025 as a decentralized response to the policies of the Trump administration. The name stands for "50 protests, 50 states, one movement," emphasizing its goal of organizing protests across all 50 states on February 5, 2025. The movement advocates for democratic values, opposes executive overreach, and supports marginalized communities. It has organized major demonstrations, including the "Hands Off!" protests on April 5, which drew millions across the U.S. The movement continues to promote civic engagement through protests, mutual aid events, and community organizing.
